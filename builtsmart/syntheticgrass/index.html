Built-Smart Synthetic Grass

Viking Pavers has the experience and wisdom of thousands of installations under their belts. Our installers are professionally trained and certified in proper installation techniques. We only recommend products that pass our high-quality standards. Combining all this is what we refer to as Built-Smart—which is unique to Viking Pavers. Don’t trust the durability, performance, and longevity of your installation to anything less.

No More Water

Built-Smart Synthetic grass is the perfect solution for dramatically lowering your monthly water bill. No watering translates to savings of up to 70% on your water bill = $500 per year for the average U.S. Household.

Virtually Maintenance-Free

Never mow, weed, or fertilize your grass ever again—just enjoy it. It will stay green all year round and for years to come. About the only thing you'll need to do is blow off any surface debris such as leaves.

Kid-Friendly, Pet-Friendly, & Eco-Friendly

The elimination of harmful pesticides and fertilizers makes it safe for you, kids, pets, and the environment. Plus, Built-Smart Synthetic Grass won't stain you kids clothes like natural grass does.

Allergy Relief

Achoo no more! Sneezing, wheezing, and itching are a thing of the past for those with allergies to traditional grass. In addition, our synthetic grass is non allergen and toxin free making it safe for you, kids, and pets.

Natural Look & Feel

Built-Smart Synthetic Grass feature a remarkable natural look and feel. Selections include a variety of colors, heights, and textures, as well as monotone and duotone styles.

Warranty

Our synthetic grass installations come with a 10-year manufacturer’s warranty and a 5-year warranty on installation. Rest assured, a synthetic grass installation by Viking Pavers is a worry-free investment for years to come.
